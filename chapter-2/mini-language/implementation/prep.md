# Mini Language

## Regular Expressions & Tokens
- Integers: `0|([1-9][0-9]*)`
- Identifiers: `[a-zA-Z_][a-zA-Z0-9_]*`
- Assignment: `!=`
- Control statements:
    - If: `if`
    - Then: `then`
    - Else: `else`
    - End: `end`
    - Repeat: `repeat`
    - Until: `until`
- Arithmetic operators:
    - Addition: `+`
    - Subtraction: `-`
    - Multiplication: `*`
    - Division: `/`
- Boolean operators:
    - Equality: `=`
    - Less than: `<`
- Other keywords:
    - Read: `read`
    - Write: `write`
- Other symbols:
    - Semicolon: `;`
    - Open brace: `{`
    - Close brace: `}`
